# 📘 Project Documentation: Notes & Tasks App for Small Teams

## 🗂️ Overview

This application is designed for small teams (2–4 people) to collaboratively manage notes, ToDos, appointments, and checklists. It follows an offline-first architecture with strong privacy protection, open-source licensing, and multi-platform support.
